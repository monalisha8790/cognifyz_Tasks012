Mandarins are small, easy-to-peel members of the Citrus genus. They are a separate species from oranges.

Apple

Apple, (Malus domestica), domesticated tree and fruit of the rose family (Rosaceae), one of the most widely cultivated tree fruits.

pomegranate

Pomegranate, (Punica granatum), bush or small tree of the family Lythraceae and its fruit.Pomegranates are low in calories and fat but high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals.

Strawberry

Strawberry, (genus Fragaria), genus of more than 20 species of flowering plants in the rose family (Rosaceae) and their edible fruit. Strawberries are native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, and cultivated varieties are widely grown throughout the world.

Papaya

Papaya, (Carica papaya), succulent fruit of a large plant of the family Caricaceae. Though its origin is rather obscure, the papaya may represent the fusion of two or more species of Carica native to Mexico and Central America.
